## What to Include

To make our first conversation productive, it helps to share:

- **Your role** and decision-making context
- **Team size** and structure (engineering, ops, platform)
- **Primary infrastructure** (cloud providers, key technologies)
- **Current pain points** (incidents, scaling, velocity, on-call burden)
- **What "boring" would look like** for your organization
- **Timeline and urgency** (exploring vs. active project)
